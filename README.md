## Introduction

MapAgent is an AI‑powered, API‑first engine that turns any application into a smart navigation assistant. It **reacts in real time** to the information you feed it—whether that’s a fresh GPS coordinate, a traffic‑congestion update, or a low‑fuel alert—so you never have to intervene manually.

### What MapAgent can do
- **Dynamic routing** – recompute the optimal path instantly when the vehicle’s location changes or when traffic conditions are updated.  
- **Context‑aware POI search** – locate nearby fuel stations, hotels, restaurants, or any point of interest on the fly.  
- **Range prediction** – calculate how far you can travel with the current fuel level, vehicle capacity, and consumption profile.  
- **Agentic integration** – expose all functionality through clean REST endpoints, allowing any UI, voice assistant, or autonomous agent to consume the service without building its own mapping logic.  

In short, MapAgent continuously processes real‑time data and delivers up‑to‑date routing and location intelligence, letting drivers stay focused on the road while the AI handles the navigation.
